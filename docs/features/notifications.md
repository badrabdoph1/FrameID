# Notifications

## Goal

Persist and deliver tenant-facing or operational notices generated by lifecycle, billing, support, publishing, or administrative workflows.

## Components

- notification records;
- tenant ownership;
- read/unread or delivery state where implemented;
- domain services that create notifications;
- customer/admin presentation surfaces.

## Data Flow

A server-side domain workflow creates a tenant-scoped notification after a meaningful event. Customer or admin views query the relevant records and update supported state through authorized mutations.

## Important Files

- notification-related modules under `src/modules/`
- notification routes/components under `src/app/`
- `prisma/schema.prisma`
- related lifecycle, billing, and support services

## Development Notes

Notifications report domain events; they must not become the owner of lifecycle, billing, or support state. Keep creation server-side, tenant-scoped, and idempotent where repeated jobs may run. Document new notification categories and delivery channels.
